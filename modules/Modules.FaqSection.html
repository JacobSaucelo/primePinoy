Frequently asked questions

Main Questions

  • How does your system work?

    Becoming a Digital Consultant is more than just a business; it is a path to personal and professional growth resulting to a successful business.

    You will have the following benefits:

    • Training on How to build a start-up digital business
    • Training on all aspects and facets of business
    • Access to Full System and Structure to ensure success
    • Assistance of Government requirements if you pass the application process
  • What are the main requirements?

    What you need to have to become accepted in this application process are the following:

    • Must have Laptop/Computer.
    • Must have a Cellphone with an internet connection.
    • Must have at least 4-5 hours daily to work in this business. (preferably 5-9 pm)
    • Must have a stable internet connection.
    • Must be committed, personally responsible, and coachable.
    • Must be of legal age. (18 years old and above)
    • Must have an interest in helping, supporting, and transforming people.
  • Are there fees?

    There are no fees in these application, selection, and mentorship processes. If you get accepted, the only fees we will be asking are "Government Legalities" like Your License.

  • How does your system work?

    We accept undergrads and graduates.
    I check based on commitment and values, not whether you have a degree or not. Everyone is welcome and will be trained.
